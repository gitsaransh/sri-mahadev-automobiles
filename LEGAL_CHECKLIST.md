## 2. Trademark Filing Checklist (India)

**Step 1: Classification**
- **Class 9:** Mobile Applications, Downloadable Software.
- **Class 42:** Software as a Service (SaaS), Technology services.

**Step 2: Preparation**
- **Applicant Name:** Saransh (Individual)
- **Address:** [Your Permanent Residential Address]
- **Brand Name:** "ReelRewards" (Wordmark)
- **Logo:** [Your App Icon/Logo File]

**Step 3: Filing Process (Online)**
1.  **Register:** Create an account on [IP India Online](https://ipindiaonline.gov.in/ tradmarksefiling/user/frmNewUserRegistration.aspx) using your Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) or standard login if applicable.
2.  **Form TM-A:** Select "New Application".
3.  **Details:**
    - Type of Applicant: Individual / Sole Proprietor.
    - Category: Small Enterprise / Individual (Fee: ₹4,500).
4.  **Upload Documents:**
    - Usage Affidavit (Claiming usage from Jan 24, 2026, or "Proposed to be used").
    - Logo Image (if filing Device mark).
5.  **Payment:** Pay ₹4,500 via Net Banking/UPI.
6.  **Receipt:** Save the Temporary Number (begins with T) and Application Number.

**Timeline:**
- **Status "Formalities Chk Pass":** ~2 weeks
- **Examination Report:** ~1-3 months
- **Journal Publication:** ~4-6 months (if no objection)
- **Registration Certificate:** ~8-12 months

**Note:** You can use the **™** symbol immediately after filing the application. Use **®** only after receiving the Registration Certificate.
