# Privacy Policy for ReelsReward

**Last Updated: January 22, 2026**

ReelsReward ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
When you register or use our App, we may ask for specific personal information to provide our services:
*   **Phone Number:** Used for authentication (via OTP) and unique account identification.
*   **Name:** Used to personalize your user profile.
*   **City/Location:** Used to provide relevant local rewards or contest leaderboards.
*   **Payment Information (UPI ID):** Collected solely for the purpose of processing reward payouts. We do not store your banking passwords or PINs.

### B. Usage and Application Data
We collect data on how you interact with our App to improve functionality and user experience:
*   **Points Balance & Transaction History:** To maintain your reward ledger.
*   **App Activity:** Feature usage (e.g., video watches, spin wheel frequency) using Firebase Analytics.
*   **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for debugging and fraud prevention.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings.
    *   [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
*   **Firebase (Analytics & Authentication):** We use Firebase (by Google) for secure user authentication, database storage, and analytics to understand app usage trends.
    *   [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
*   **RazorPay / UPI:** We may use RazorPay or direct UPI integration to facilitate reward payouts. Your payment details are processed securely by these financial providers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
*   To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (e.g., tracking streaks and rewards).
*   To process your payout requests efficiently.
*   To display relevant advertisements via AdMob.
*   To prevent fraud, abuse, and security breaches.
*   To notify you about updates, duel invites, or service changes.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide you with our services.
*   **User Rights:** You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
*   **How to Request Deletion:** Please contact us at the email provided below with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will delete your data within 30 days of verification.

## 5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
